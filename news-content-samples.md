## Article Structure Templates

### Standard News Article
```
Headline: [Compelling, descriptive title]
Lead Paragraph: [Who, What, When, Where, Why - key facts]
Body Paragraphs: 
- Background information
- Expert quotes
- Additional details
- Impact and implications
Conclusion: [Summary or call to action]
```

### Breaking News Format
```
BREAKING: [Urgent headline]
[Location, Date] - [Quick summary of key facts]
This is a developing story. More details to follow.
Last Updated: [Timestamp]
```

### Feature Article Template
```
Engaging Headline
Subtitle: [Additional context]
Byline: By [Author Name]
Date: [Publication date]

Introduction: [Hook to grab reader attention]
Main Content: [Detailed exploration of topic]
Expert Analysis: [Quotes from authorities]
Conclusion: [Final thoughts or implications]
```

## SEO-Friendly Headlines

### Examples of Engaging Headlines
- "BREAKING: [Event] Shakes [Industry/Location]"
- "[Number] Things You Need to Know About [Topic]"
- "[Authority Figure] Reveals Shocking Truth About [Subject]"
- "Why [Event/Decision] Could Change Everything"
- "[Location] Sees Historic [Event/Achievement]"
- "Exclusive: [Person/Organization] Speaks Out on [Issue]"

### Headline Best Practices
- Keep under 60 characters for SEO
- Include relevant keywords
- Create urgency or curiosity
- Use active voice
- Include numbers when possible
- Avoid clickbait that misleads
